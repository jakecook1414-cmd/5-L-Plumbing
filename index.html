Our Story

Where Integrity Meets Expertise

5L Plumbing LLC is a family-owned and operated business built on the belief that every customer deserves honest work at a fair price.

Founded by Keaton Long — a licensed Master Plumber with over 20 years in the trade — 5L Plumbing has been serving the Ponder area and surrounding Denton & Wise County communities with the kind of craftsmanship and care that only a family business can provide.
